WeConnect

We aim to create a platform in the form of both an app and website evolved around the concept of community and togetherness, which essentially allows individuals with ADHD to connect and talk with those who can help them. Our solution places great emphasis on the concept of building community, as it includes many features that bring individuals together and remind them that they are not alone in this. The main features of the platform is built around a “pen-pal” idea, in which individuals with ADHD are paired up with another person whom they can talk to and message. The pairs are created according to similar interests and hobbies. As not everyone may have access to the internet, the app, which can run offline, proves to be convenient in this case.

Features

Weekly Webinars

This section permits the participation of the patients in weekly webinars that aim to provide them with adequate information regarding their disorder, and to create individualised plans of improvement.

Meet A Doctor

Our App would offer the ability to directly contact professionals from the sector. It is extremely important for patients to be provided with adequate medical support, that would give them the right suggestions and advice to cope with the symptoms.

Gratitude Wall

This feature permits the patients to feel a sense of gratitude over what they achieved in their lives and of the support that they received from the people surrounding them. Through this feature, however, they can also express their gratutitude to the patients they knew with the App, and so let them know that they are important. This will increase their self-esteem.

Talk To A Bot

Have a word with a bot, that can help you assess your current mental heealth condition, and help improve it.

Guided Meditation

The basic concept of meditation is that it is a practice that connects the mind and the body. Its purpose is to help increase both physical and mental peace and calm, which also helps you to learn how to live more fully in the present.

Read Our Blog

Read our blog that will give you tips and tricks to maintain a better mental health and frame of mind.
